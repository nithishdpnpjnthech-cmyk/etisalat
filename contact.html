Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we receive.

How quickly can I get Etisalat internet installed? +
Installation is typically completed within 2–5 business days after order confirmation. Our team will contact you to schedule a convenient time.
What business internet plans are available? +
We offer Business Starter Pro (1 Gbps, AED 279/month), Business Ultimate 200Mbps and 400Mbps fiber plans, and 5G Wireless plans. All include free installation and optional landline services.
Can I port my existing number to Etisalat? +
Yes! We offer 3 months free porting on most business plans. Contact our sales team and we will handle the entire process for you.
Is there a contract commitment required? +
Most plans require a 12-month commitment to get the best pricing and priority support. Contact us for flexible options if needed.
